Introduction

HyperPin is next-generation local delivery startup founded by Yash Bhardwaj in Godda, Jharkhand. The name 'HyperPin' blends the concepts of hyperlocal service and PIN code targetting, reflecting our goal of serving every neighborhood quickly and reliably.

Built with a focus on small-town convenience, HyperPin bridges the gap between local vendors and customers by offering a fast, tech-enabled grocery delivery platform. Whether it's vegetables, fruits, or daily essentials, we ensure items are delivered fresh and on time-wiothout the complexity or pricing of larger platforms.

We are proud to launch our journey from Godda, starting small but aiming big ,with a long-term goal of transforming how people shop for essentials across Jharkhand.
